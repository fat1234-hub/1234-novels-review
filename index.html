Velvet Tomes

Introduction

This website is dedicated to presenting and reviewing novels. Here, you will find summaries, main ideas, and notable quotes from various novels. In the future, the website will be developed to allow users to discuss novels together. The novels featured are primarily in Arabic, but if users wish, we will provide translations into their preferred languages. In the future, as the website develops, we can also publish users' novels upon their request.

Development

This website was created by a beginner, and I have many ideas for its development. In this section, I will write down all the ideas and features that I hope to add to the website in the future as it grows and improves:

  • Create a sleek, smooth and easy-to-use interface.
  • Add a chat for users to discuss the novel.
  • Enable the user to publish his novels.
  • Adding the feature of translating novels into the user's preferred language
  • Adding artificial intelligence to help the user
  • Add a logo to the website
  • Add site settings
  • Add a search feature to find novels quickly

Thank you for visiting the site, and I hope you enjoy
